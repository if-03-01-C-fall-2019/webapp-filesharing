Mega

Motto: The Privacy Company

Mega (Recursive acronym for Mega Encrypted Global Access) was realised in August 2013. Originally developed by Kim Dotcom whho sold the website in 2015.
the idea was to make a website that can encrypt your data as good as possible. Now Mega has more than 100 milion
active users and a gain of 263 million US-Dollars per year.

Fun fact: if you find a coding mistake, you will get round about 10.000 US-Dollars.
