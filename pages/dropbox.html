Dropbox

Dropbox was realised in june 2007. Developt by Dropbox Inc. The most latest version supports Windows, Linux and MacOS. It was programed in python an C++. Dropbox had approximately 12.7 milion customers in 2018 and made about 1,39 billions US-Dollars. Most people just store data for themself without publishing it (cloud) but you can also share your files with an simple link. Your free 2 GB can easily be expanded by paying most commonly 10 to 15 euros per month
